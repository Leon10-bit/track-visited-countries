My Favorite Countries

USA

I love the diversity and national parks of the USA. There's always something new to explore, from coast to coast.

France

France's art, history, and cuisine are simply unparalleled. Wandering the streets of Paris is a dream come true.

Japan

Japan blends ancient tradition with cutting-edge technology. Cherry blossom season is absolutely magical.

Brazil

The energy of Rio, the rhythms of samba, and the beauty of the Amazon make Brazil a must-visit destination.

Canada

From the Rocky Mountains to vibrant cities like Toronto and Montreal, Canada offers endless natural beauty and culture.
